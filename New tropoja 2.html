About Tropoja

Albania's Mountain Paradise

Nestled in the Albanian Alps in the northernmost part of Albania, Tropoja is a region of breathtaking natural beauty. With its dramatic mountain landscapes, crystal-clear rivers, and traditional villages, Tropoja offers visitors an authentic glimpse into Albanian mountain culture.

The area is known for its warm hospitality, delicious local cuisine, and rich folklore traditions. Whether you're looking for adventure, cultural experiences, or simply a peaceful retreat in nature, Tropoja has something to offer every traveler.

The region gained international recognition after being featured in the movie "Taken," but the real Tropoja is a place of stunning beauty and cultural richness that goes far beyond cinema depictions.

Top Attractions

Valbona Valley National Park

Experience one of Albania's most spectacular natural wonders with pristine alpine landscapes, crystal-clear rivers, and diverse wildlife.

Valbona to Theth Hike

This iconic hiking trail takes you through some of the most beautiful mountain scenery in the Balkans, connecting two remarkable alpine villages.

Bajram Curri

The main town of Tropoja offers a glimpse into local life with its colorful markets, traditional architecture, and authentic Albanian cuisine.

Ragam Waterfall

A spectacular natural wonder where water cascades down rocky cliffs surrounded by lush vegetation and alpine meadows.

Traditional Villages

Explore charming mountain villages where time seems to stand still, offering authentic cultural experiences and warm Albanian hospitality.

Komani Lake

Take a boat trip through the fjord-like Komani Lake with its turquoise waters and dramatic mountain scenery on all sides.
